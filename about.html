We focus on delivering high quality user experience design

We love to design challenging digital products.

AK Studio was founded in 2019 by Aftab Khalid. A UX Designer himself, Aftab aimed to build a company which can provide design services with purpose and meaning. In a short span of time, AK Studio has delivered remarkable product design and user experience services to clients across different industries and regions.

We were able to cultivate a multi-disciplinary team of UX strategists, UX designers, visual artists, interface designers and content writers. Our capabilities are in producing web and mobile designs - especially for enterprise apps with multi-dimensional use cases and complex data including SaaS, Data Visualization, Ecommerce, and Fintech industry.
